Structural Workforce Plan

AI-Powered Future (2025-2027)

Executive Summary

This document outlines a strategic workforce plan for WCT Holdings Berhad to navigate the future of work by integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) into its operations. The core objective is to enhance productivity and streamline processes, leading to a planned headcount reduction of approximately 10% over the next 18 months. This reduction will be achieved entirely through natural attrition, with no layoffs. The focus of this transformation will be on administrative-heavy functions within the Finance, Property Management, and Human Resources departments. By embracing automation, WCT is not only aiming to improve margins but also to foster a more agile, skilled, and future-ready workforce.

Strategic Goals (2025-2027)

Productivity

Increase operational efficiency by 20% in targeted functions.

AI Adoption

Achieve 90% adoption rate of AI tools among relevant employees.

Service Levels

Maintain or exceed 95% SLA compliance in all transitioning departments.

Headcount

Reduce headcount by ~10% (~140 roles) via natural attrition.

Reskilling

Redeploy at least 30 employees into new, high-value digital roles.
